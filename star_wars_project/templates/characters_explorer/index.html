Star Wars Characters Explorer

Star Wars characters explorer is a simple application allowing you to fetch collections containing all of the saga's characters.
Once you fetch the latests collection you can explore data about the characters.
Just go to the collection tab and fetch the data.

The data is provided by the SWAPI
You can visit their website at SWAPI
